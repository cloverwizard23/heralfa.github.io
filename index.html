Welcome to my site

Hello, my name is Heralf. I'm a college student at UCR. I initially started as a Bioengineering major, focusing on medical devices but then added Biology as my second major when I realized there was overlap and I could still graduate in time. My interests in these fields have evolved to be in bioinformatics, specifically genetic sequencing and phenotype expression, and the creation of devices that can help researchers and non-academics to explore, treat, and learn more about genetic sequences.
